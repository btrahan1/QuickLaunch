# QuickLaunch 🪐🎙️

QuickLaunch is a premium, mobile-first utility that turns your voice into a universal search for your phone. Use it to launch any app instantly, from Netflix and Spotify to Mail and Amazon.

It is designed for "Input-to-Action in < 3 seconds." 

## ✨ Features

### 🎙️ Unified Voice Hub
One giant central microphone button for all your commands:
- **"Netflix"** -> Instantly launches the Netflix app.
- **"Mail"** -> Opens your primary email app.
- **"YouTube"** -> Jumps straight to the video feed.

### 🧠 Smart Logic
- **Fuzzy Matching**: Intelligently handles voice variations (e.g., "Face book" or "Insta").
- **Launch Countdown**: Provides a premium 1-second "Launching..." countdown with a sleek progress bar.
- **Cancel Button**: A quick window to stop a launch if the wrong app is heard.

### 🍱 Cinematic Design
- **Glassmorphism Theme**: Sleek dark mode with glowing blue and purple accents.
- **PWA Ready**: Install as a standalone "Remote Control" for your phone.

### 🛠️ Total Customization
- **Add Custom App**: Save your own custom URI schemes (`myapp://`) directly to LocalStorage.
- **25+ Built-in Apps**: Pre-configured for the most popular streaming, social, and utility apps.
